Privacy Policy

WalkpadLog - Android App

Short Version: WalkpadLog is a privacy-first app. Your walking data is stored locally on your device. We do not use any external servers, and we do not sell your data.

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WalkpadLog ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Collection and Use

We collect and process the following types of data to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Physical Activity Data: Distance walked and step counts that you manually enter or that are calculated based on your settings.
  • App Settings: Your preferences such as unit system (metric/imperial), step length, and reminder times.

2. Health Connect Integration

WalkpadLog offers integration with Google Health Connect. If you explicitly enable this feature:

  • Writing Data: We export your walking sessions (distance, steps, and timestamps) as "Workouts" to Health Connect. This allows you to sync your WalkpadLog data with other health apps.
  • Read Access: We do not read any data from your Health Connect storage.
  • Limited Use Requirement: WalkpadLog's use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the Health Connect Permissions policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Storage

All data collected by the App is stored locally on your mobile device using an internal database (Room). We do not transmit this data to any external servers or cloud services controlled by us.

4. Notifications

If you grant permission, the App sends local notifications to remind you of your daily walks. These notifications are processed entirely on your device.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual walking sessions or all your data at once within the App's settings ("Delete All Data"). Uninstalling the App will also remove all locally stored data.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties, except for the explicit export to Google Health Connect if enabled by you. We do not use advertising or analytics frameworks that track your behavior.
